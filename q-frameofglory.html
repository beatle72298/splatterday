Can I Get My Pic on the Frame of Glory?

Yes. Yes you can. Anyone who scores in the top ten of any Splatterday tournament earns one of the highest accolades bestowed by The Knight School. They are named a “Knight School National All-Star.”
The parent of any All-Star may email a pic to Dr. Brooks at dbrooksphd@theknightschool.com and the All-Star will see their shining face up on the Frame of Glory the following week. This is a fun way to view the people we routinely play and routinely chat with, so it makes Splatterdays all the more social! Newer pics are placed at the top.
